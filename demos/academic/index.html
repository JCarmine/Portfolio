About

An academic portfolio is the perfect way to establish an online presence and share your work with the world. Your portfolio will give you an easy way to provide an overview of your studies, information about your teaching experience or research, and provide a place where visitors can view and download your CV. In addition, you can also share a list of your publications, and provide links to the journals or repositories where they are shared online.

The Academic Portfolio features:

  • A clean and non-distracting design that will allow your visitors to easily find the information they need.
  • A fully responsive design that will ensure it looks great everywhere, even on smart phones.
  • Clean and well organized code that adheres to the most modern web standards including HTML5 and CSS3.
  • The brand new Google Maps API that will allow visitors to easily find your office or lab.
  • An easily customizable design that will allow it to be quickly personalized and modified to suit your needs.
  • Social media links that will drive visitors to your presence on LinkedIn or other social media sites.
